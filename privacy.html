Privacy Policy

Social Post Studio is a private tool for preparing and managing social media posts.

The app may request permission to connect to social media accounts so the user can prepare, publish, or schedule their own posts.

Account tokens and post data are stored locally on the user's computer unless the user chooses to send a post to a connected platform.

Social Post Studio does not sell personal data.

Users can revoke access at any time through the connected platform's app settings.
